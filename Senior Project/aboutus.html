About Us

Welcome to Kean University Marketplace! We are dedicated to providing a platform for Kean University students, faculty, and alumni to buy and sell goods and services within the university community.

Our mission is to create a convenient and safe environment for transactions, fostering collaboration and resourcefulness among members of the Kean community.

Whether you're looking to buy textbooks, sell electronics, or offer tutoring services, Kean University Marketplace is your go-to destination.
